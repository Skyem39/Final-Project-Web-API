Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions and answers

How do I register a new club?

Navigate to the "Register Club" page from the sidebar, fill in the required details including club name, category, description, and advisor name, then submit the form.

Can students join multiple clubs?

Yes, students can join as many clubs as they wish by completing the member registration form for each club.

What are the membership types?

There are three types: Regular Member (general participation), Committee Member (assists in club activities), and President (leads the club).

How do I view the dashboard analytics?

The main dashboard displays real-time statistics, trend indicators, bar charts showing club distribution, and recent club listings. All data updates automatically from the backend.

How do I use the search functionality?

Use the search bar in the top navigation to quickly find clubs or members across the system.
