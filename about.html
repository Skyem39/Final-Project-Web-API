About the System

Enterprise Club Management Platform

Club Registration Portal

The Student Club Management System enables efficient registration and administration of student organizations. This platform facilitates club creation, member enrollment, and comprehensive reporting for educational institutions.

Core Features

  • Club Registration: Register new student organizations with complete details including name, category, description, and faculty advisor information.
  • Member Management: Add students to clubs with tracking of membership types (Regular, Committee, President).
  • Real-time Statistics: View live data on total clubs, active members, and participation metrics.
  • Comprehensive Reports: Generate detailed reports showing all clubs and their registered members.
  • Category Organization: Clubs are organized into categories: Academic, Sports, Arts & Culture, Community Service, and Technology.
